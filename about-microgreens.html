About Microgreens

Understanding the superfood stage of plant growth

What Are Microgreens?

Microgreens are edible seedlings of vegetables and herbs, harvested shortly after the first true leaves (cotyledons) have fully developed. They represent a unique "superfood" stage of plant growth‚Äîcapturing plants at their peak nutrient density, right before anti-nutrients begin to form.

Unlike sprouts (which are germinated seeds eaten whole, including roots), microgreens are cut just above the soil line and consist only of the stem and tender young leaves. This makes them safer, more flavorful, and more versatile in culinary applications.

Common microgreen varieties include sunflower, broccoli, radish, pea shoots, basil, kale, arugula, and many more. Each offers distinct flavors, colors, and nutritional profiles.

Seed-to-Harvest Process

Our entire growth cycle takes just 14‚Äì21 days in total, from seed selection to delivery. Every step is carefully controlled to ensure optimal quality, nutrition, and safety.

1

Seed Selection

We choose high-quality, organic seeds for each crop. Seed quality is critical‚Äîwe source only from trusted suppliers to ensure purity and germination rates.

2

Sowing

Seeds are evenly distributed in growing trays filled with organic soil or a nutrient-rich growing medium. Proper density ensures healthy, uniform growth.

3

Germination (2-3 Days)

Trays are placed in a controlled environment with optimal warmth and moisture. Seeds absorb water, swell, and begin to sprout. This is a critical phase where conditions must be carefully monitored.

4

Growth (7-14 Days)

Once sprouted, seedlings are exposed to LED grow lights and maintained in a climate-controlled environment. Temperature, humidity, and light intensity are optimized for each variety. During this phase, the plants develop their first true leaves and reach peak nutrient density.

5

Harvest

Microgreens are carefully snipped just above the soil line using clean, sanitized tools. Timing is everything‚Äîwe harvest at the exact moment when flavor and nutrition are at their peak.

6

Packaging & Delivery

Freshly harvested greens are gently washed, packaged in eco-friendly, compostable containers, and delivered the same day. From our rooftop farm to your kitchen in hours‚Äînot days.

The Science Behind the Superfood

Microgreens are considered a "superfood stage" because research shows they retain exceptionally high levels of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants during early growth. As plants mature, they often produce anti-nutrients (compounds that inhibit nutrient absorption), but microgreens are harvested before this occurs.

Studies have documented that microgreens can contain up to 40 times more nutrients than their mature counterparts. For instance:

  • Red cabbage microgreens have 40√ó more Vitamin E than mature cabbage.
  • Broccoli microgreens contain extremely high levels of sulforaphane, a compound linked to cancer prevention and detoxification.
  • Radish microgreens are packed with vitamin C and antioxidants that support immune health.

This makes microgreens an ideal ingredient for health-conscious consumers, wellness-focused businesses, and anyone looking to maximize nutrition in every bite.
