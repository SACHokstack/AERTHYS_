Our Team

Experts in sustainable agriculture and microgreens cultivation

Aerthys is built on a foundation of expertise, passion, and a shared commitment to sustainable agriculture. Our team combines deep knowledge of horticulture with hands-on farming experience to deliver the highest quality microgreens.

Theivanth Rajamohan

Founder & Microgreens Specialist

Theivanth is the visionary behind Aerthys. He grew up on his grandmother's farm, where he developed a deep appreciation for sustainable agriculture. After studying at Singapore Management University and specializing in sustainability, Theivanth returned to India with a mission: to create a local, pesticide-free food system that nourishes both people and the planet.

With expertise in controlled-environment agriculture and urban farming, Theivanth oversees all aspects of Aerthys's operations‚Äîfrom seed selection to customer delivery. His commitment to quality and sustainability drives every decision at Aerthys.

Dr. Shanmugasundaram

Agricultural Scientist & Advisor

Dr. Shanmugasundaram is an Agricultural Scientist at Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) with over 20 years of experience in horticulture research and microgreens cultivation. His deep expertise in plant science and sustainable farming practices has been instrumental in shaping Aerthys's growing protocols.

As a trusted advisor to Aerthys, Dr. Shanmugasundaram provides guidance on crop selection, optimal growing conditions, and best practices for maximizing nutrient density and yield. His scientific rigor ensures that every batch of microgreens meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
