Our Microgreens

Fresh, nutrient-dense varieties grown for flavor and wellness

At Aerthys, we cultivate a diverse range of microgreens tailored to meet the needs of Coimbatore's restaurants, hotels, and wellness businesses. Each variety is carefully selected for its nutritional profile, flavor, and versatility in culinary applications.

All our products are: Pesticide-free • Harvested fresh daily • Locally grown • Delivered within hours

Our Delivery Model

At Aerthys, freshness is paramount. We harvest your microgreens to order and deliver them to your door or kitchen within hours. Our hyper-local model ensures you receive produce at peak nutrition and flavor.

  • Same-Day Harvest & Delivery: Orders are fulfilled and delivered within Coimbatore on the same day.
  • Custom Orders: We work directly with chefs and businesses to tailor varieties and quantities to your needs.
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging: All packaging is compostable or recyclable to minimize environmental impact.
  • Consistent Supply: Our controlled growing environment ensures year-round availability.

Our product selection varies based on local demand and seasonal preferences. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
