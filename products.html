Our Microgreens

Fresh, nutrient-dense varieties grown for flavor and wellness. Harvested at dawn. Delivered in hours.

At Aerthys, we cultivate a diverse range of microgreens tailored to meet the needs of Coimbatore's restaurants, hotels, and wellness businesses. Each variety is carefully selected for its nutritional profile, flavor, and versatility in culinary applications.

All our products are: Pesticide-free • Harvested fresh daily • Locally grown • Delivered within hours

Sunflower Microgreens

Crunchy, nutty greens with a mild, pleasant flavor that works beautifully in salads, sandwiches, and as a garnish.

  • Rich in plant protein and healthy fats
  • High in Vitamin E and zinc
  • Supports gut health with dietary fiber
  • Crunchy texture adds depth to any dish

Broccoli Microgreens

Mild, earthy greens packed with one of nature's most powerful antioxidants.

  • Extremely high in sulforaphane, a potent anti-cancer compound
  • Rich in vitamins A, C, and K
  • Supports detoxification and immune function
  • Subtle flavor works in smoothies and salads

Radish Microgreens

Spicy, peppery greens that add a bold kick to any plate.

  • Packed with vitamins A, C, and minerals
  • Natural digestive aid
  • Adds vibrant color and crunch
  • Popular in Asian cuisine and fusion dishes

Wheatgrass

The classic superfood green, perfect for juicing and wellness shots.

  • Rich in chlorophyll and antioxidants
  • Alkalizing and detoxifying
  • High in vitamins A, C, and E
  • Popular in juice bars and wellness centers

Basil Microgreens

Aromatic, flavorful greens with the classic basil taste in a delicate form.

  • Intense basil flavor and aroma
  • Rich in antioxidants and essential oils
  • Perfect for Italian and Mediterranean dishes
  • Beautiful as a garnish

Pea Shoots

Sweet, tender shoots with a fresh pea flavor and crisp texture.

  • High in vitamins A, C, and folate
  • Naturally sweet taste
  • Tender texture ideal for salads and stir-fries
  • Supports heart and eye health
