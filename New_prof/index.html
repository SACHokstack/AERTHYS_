Who Are We

Aerthys Farms is built on a simple belief: real health begins with real food.

We grow microgreens as a daily source of natural nutrition for people and kitchens that care about what goes into the body. No chemicals. No shortcuts. No exaggerated health claims.

Our approach is rooted in sustainability, freshness, and honesty. Every harvest is timed for peak nutrition, every batch grown with intention, and every delivery handled with care.

Aerthys is not about chasing wellness trends or selling quick fixes. It's about rebuilding nutrition the natural way, one meal at a time.

Founder Story

Aerthys Farms was founded by Theivanth Rajamohan, whose work and studies in sustainability shaped a deep interest in how food systems affect long-term health.

While pursuing his Master's in Management with a focus on Sustainability at Singapore Management University, he experienced food ecosystems where clean, nutrient-rich food was a daily standard. Returning to India revealed a growing dependence on artificial supplements and chemically treated produce.

This contrast led to a simple question: What if food itself could become the foundation of health again?

The answer became microgreens‚Äîfresh, nutrient-dense, and naturally integrated into everyday meals. Aerthys Farms was created to rebuild trust in food through clean, honest nutrition.
