Our Story

"We're not just growing microgreens—we're reimagining urban agriculture for Coimbatore."

— Theivanth Rajamohan, Founder

Founded with a vision to transform how Coimbatore thinks about fresh, sustainable food. We're bridging the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern urban innovation, delivering the city's freshest microgreens straight from rooftop to your table.

Theivanth Rajamohan

Founder & CEO

From Village Roots to Urban Innovation

The Beginning

Growing up spending summers at his grandmother's traditional farm, Theivanth learned the timeless principles of working with nature rather than against it. These formative experiences planted the seeds of understanding.

The Vision

During his studies at Singapore Management University, a sustainability program reignited his agricultural passion. He realized the future of food production lay in revolutionizing how and where we grow our food.

The Mission

Today, Theivanth leads Aerthys with a mission to bring ultra-fresh, nutrient-dense microgreens directly from Coimbatore's rooftops to local restaurants, hotels, and conscious consumers.

Seeding

Day 4-7

Growth Monitoring

Daily monitoring of temperature, humidity, and nutrients to ensure optimal growing conditions and healthy development.

Day 8-14

Harvest Window
