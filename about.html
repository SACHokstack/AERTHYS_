Our Founder's Journey

Aerthys was born from a deep connection to the land and a vision for sustainable agriculture. Our founder, Theivanth Rajamohan, spent his childhood at his grandmother's village farm, where he learned the rhythms of nature and the value of working with the earth.

Years later, while studying at Singapore Management University, Theivanth participated in a sustainability program that rekindled his passion for agriculture. He recognized the urgent need for farming practices that nourish both people and the planet.
