About Aerthys

Growing sustainably, delivering freshly

Our Founder's Story

Aerthys was born from a deep connection to the land and a vision for sustainable agriculture. Our founder, Theivanth Rajamohan, spent his childhood at his grandmother's village farm, where he learned the rhythms of nature and the value of working with the earth.

Years later, while studying at Singapore Management University, Theivanth participated in a sustainability program that rekindled his passion for agriculture. He recognized the urgent need for farming practices that nourish both people and the planet. This realization led him to specialize in sustainable agriculture and ultimately to establish Aerthys.

Theivanth set out with a clear mission: to grow pesticide-free microgreens that promote consumer well-being while minimizing environmental impact. Today, Aerthys operates from rooftop farms in Coimbatore, bringing ultra-fresh, nutrient-dense greens to local restaurants, hotels, and wellness businesses.

Our Growth Plans

Aerthys is just getting started. We're scaling from our pilot rooftop farm to expand into neighboring cities across Tamil Nadu and beyond. Our roadmap includes:

  • Launching business subscription models for reliable, recurring deliveries
  • Expanding our product line with new microgreen varieties tailored to regional tastes
  • Investing in R&D for value-added products like microgreen powders and ready-to-eat blends
  • Building partnerships with more restaurants, hotels, and wellness centers
  • Educating communities about the benefits of urban farming and sustainable food systems

We're committed to making sustainable, nutritious food accessible to all, one rooftop at a time.
