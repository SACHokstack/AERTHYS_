Fresh, Local, and Sustainable

Founded by Theivanth Rajamohan, Aerthys brings together a passion for sustainable agriculture and a commitment to providing the freshest, most nutritious microgreens to Coimbatore's food and wellness industry. Our rooftop farm produces pesticide-free greens year-round, delivered within hours of harvest to ensure peak freshness and flavor.

We believe in farming that works with nature, not against it. By growing locally and sustainably, we reduce food miles, conserve water, and deliver produce that is better for you and better for the planet.
